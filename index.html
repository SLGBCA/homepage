About us

Sobha Lake Gardens Bengali Cultural Association (SLGBCA)

The Sobha Lake Gardens Bengali Cultural Association (SLGBCA) is a cultural and social group established by the Bengali families of Sobha Lake Gardens. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate Bengali culture while fostering a spirit of inclusiveness. SLGBCA not only upholds the rich traditions of Bengali heritage but also embraces the diversity within our community. We come together with many enthusiastic and dedicated volunteers to organize and celebrate a variety of festivals, sharing the joy with friends and families residing at SLG.

SLGBCA will organise festivals such as Durga Puja, Kali Puja, Saraswati Puja, and Rabindra Jayanti (Birthday of Rabindra Nath Tagore) in upcoming year at SLG
